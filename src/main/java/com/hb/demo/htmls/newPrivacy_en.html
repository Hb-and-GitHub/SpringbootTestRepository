1. During your use of the Clinflash eConsent App services, we will collect the information you voluntarily provide or that is generated as a result of your use of the services, in order to provide you with services or optimize our services.

Account Registration: Offline doctors and assistants collect your phone number or email to help you open a Clinflash eConsent account.

App Login: When you log in to Clinflash eConsent App, we will obtain your mobile phone device information (including but not limited to the information describing the basic situation of personal commonly used devices, including hardware serial numbers and IMEI/Android ID unique device identification codes). In addition, we will obtain your MAC information in the background state of App, so that we can adapt to different device models and provide users with differentiated interface display content. Message Push: The App may obtain your MAC address information through data collection and/or in-app advertising, limited to Android phones. MAC address information is considered sensitive, and we do not actively collect such information without your explicit authorization. If you do not provide this information, it may affect our ability to provide you with more accurate services, but it will not affect your normal use of basic App platform browsing and other functions.

Real-name Authentication: In order to ensure the security and authenticity of user accounts, we utilize third-party real-name authentication services. User personal information, including name, ID number, phone number, and facial biometric information, is only used for real-name authentication and will not be used for any other purpose. We will strictly protect the security of user personal information and establish reasonable information confidentiality agreements with the real-name authentication service provider.

3. We will use the collected information for the purposes covered by this privacy policy.
